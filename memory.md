## Messaging — What Works / What Doesn't

### Lead with the SEO data layer, not connectors
Connectors (GSC, GA4, Bing) feel like table stakes to power users — especially those already on Make or AirOps. The real differentiator is two things working together: **(1) the SEO data layer is native and pre-loaded** — no per-client OAuth wiring, no setup; and **(2) the workflow builder starts from SEO context**, not general automation you have to teach SEO to. That's a product category difference, not a feature comparison.

### ICP-1 messaging anchor
**"Build once, all clients, no setup."** Lead with the operational multiplier. Avoid feature lists.

### "Early stage" framing — when to use it
Avoid in cold outreach to Director-level and above. It signals risk to people accountable for client workflows. Reserve for warm follow-up once genuine curiosity is established.
